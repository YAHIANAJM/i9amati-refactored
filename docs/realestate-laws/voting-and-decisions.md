# Voting and Decisions — Loi 18-00

## Decision-making bodies

| Body | Scope |
|------|-------|
| Assemblée Générale (AG) | All decisions within a single residence/syndicat |
| Conseil Syndical | Decisions about global common areas in a complex |

---

## Voting thresholds (AG)

### Simple / relative majority (Article 20)
Decisions affecting day-to-day management. Valid even at second session (no quorum required).

Covers:
- Maintaining the building and ensuring security/quiet enjoyment
- Authorizing a co-owner to carry out works on common areas at their own expense
- Installing shared antennas or equipment
- Arranging spaces for religious rituals
- Accessibility measures for persons with disabilities
- Hiring, dismissing, and setting conditions for the concierge (*gardien*)

### 3/4 majority of all co-owners (Article 21)
Structural and governance decisions. Must be taken at a validly quorate meeting.

Covers:
- Drafting or modifying the *règlement de copropriété*
- Improvement works (changing or adding equipment)
- **Electing and revoking the syndic and adjoint** ← critical
- Revising the distribution of common area shares
- **Setting the syndic's fees/salary**
- **Approving the budget**, fixing charges, ceiling on expenses, and the maintenance reserve
- Major maintenance works
- Taking out collective insurance
- Granting mandate to the syndic or third parties for specific actions
- Establishing a right of first refusal (*droit de préférence*) — only for complexes with ≤ 20 TFs (Article 39)
- Partial demolition of the building

### Unanimity (Article 22)
Irreversible structural changes:

Covers:
- Construction of structures for individual use in common areas
- Transfer of the right of elevation or creating new buildings in already completed areas
- Works modifying the common areas that benefit only some co-owners
- Converting common areas to private use (except those defined by Art. 4)
- Granting elevation or excavation rights
- Total demolition of the building

---

## Conseil Syndical decisions (Article 29 bis)

Decisions taken by **absolute majority** of syndics and adjoints of all sub-syndicats, present or represented at the council meeting.

Covers:
- All matters relating to global common areas of the complex
- Budget and charges for global common areas (allocated to each sub-syndicat proportionally — Article 29 quater)

---

## Votes and quote-parts

- Each co-owner has votes proportional to their **quote-part indivise** (Article 16 decies)
- A co-owner holding more than half the total votes is automatically capped at **50% of the remaining votes**
- A proxy holder cannot represent more than **3 co-owners** and their combined shares cannot exceed **10%** of all votes
- Co-owners in **charge default** may not attend or vote (Article 16 quinquies)

---

## Second session rule (Article 18)

| Session | Quorum required | Majority to pass |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|
| First | 50% of co-owners present/represented | As per decision type above |
| Second (within 30 days) | None | Relative majority of those present |

Note: Art. 21 (3/4) and Art. 22 (unanimity) decisions cannot be downgraded at the second session — they still require their full threshold.

---

## Contesting a decision (Article 59 duodecies)

Any co-owner or injured party can challenge an AG decision at the *tribunal de première instance* within **2 months** of notification.

## Quick reference: decision → required majority

| Decision | Majority |
|----------|---------|
| Day-to-day maintenance | Simple |
| Hire/fire gardien | Simple |
| Install shared antennas | Simple |
| Elect/revoke syndic | 3/4 |
| Approve annual budget | 3/4 |
| Fix syndic compensation | 3/4 |
| Major renovation works | 3/4 |
| Modify *règlement de copropriété* | 3/4 |
| Convert common area to private | Unanimity |
| Total demolition | Unanimity |
| Sell right of elevation | Unanimity |
| Conseil syndical decisions | Absolute majority of syndics + adjoints |
